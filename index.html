Drawing Pair Rating Study

A short study for a research-methods course at UdK / TU Berlin · ~15–20 minutes

What this is. You will look at 40 pairs of drawing strokes. For each pair you are asked to rate, on a scale of 1 to 5, how strongly the second stroke appears to respond to the first.

What you will see. Each pair is shown as three panels side by side:

  • Context (before) — the state of the drawing before either stroke was added, shown in grey.
  • Stroke A — the first stroke, in black.
  • Stroke B — the next stroke, in black.

Your task. Ask yourself: does Stroke B seem to be a reaction to Stroke A? There is no right answer. Use your intuition. The whole point is that we want your reading, not a calculated one.

Anonymity. No personal data is collected. We only ask for a short pseudonym so we can tell ratings apart. Your ratings are stored locally in your browser until you submit them at the end.

Best done on a desktop or tablet. Please do it in one sitting if you can.
